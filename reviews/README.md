# Review records

Self-review artifacts for the policy documents: consistency reviews,
benchmark verifications, and re-review watchlists. These are records, not
working documents — each dated file is fixed once written and superseded by
a newer dated review rather than edited.

Conventions:

- **Dated reviews** are named `YYYYMMDD-<slug>.md` and are immutable.
- **Watchlists** (e.g. a re-review watchlist tracking external document
  dependencies) are the one living exception: they are updated in place and
  say so in their header.

Decisions that come out of a review belong in [`decisions/`](../decisions/)
as ADRs; open items go to
[`REMAINING_DECISIONS.md`](../REMAINING_DECISIONS.md). Research runs that
feed a review (peer benchmarks, deep-research reports) live in
[`research/`](../research/).
