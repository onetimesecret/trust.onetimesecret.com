# Research

Background, drafting material, and durable rationale records behind the
policy documents. Held here so it stays out of the top-level directory.
These notes are:

- not intended as a reference for general use; they contain gaps,
  assumptions, and internal commentary.
- not intentionally opinionated; they are a record of the drafting process
  and rationale.
- kind of like show-your-work notes for the lawyers, but not a substitute
  for the actual policy documents.

## Layout

Two kinds of file live here, and the distinction is the convention:

- **Topical rationale** (this directory, undated filenames): living
  reference documents on a subject — e.g. business arrangements, passphrase
  cryptography, the Approximated architecture. Updated in place as
  understanding improves.
- **Dated research runs** (`YYYY/YYYYMMDD-<slug>.md`): point-in-time
  records of a specific research effort — e.g. a peer benchmark or
  deep-research report. Immutable once written; a follow-up run gets a new
  dated file that links back.

`revision-history-2026.md` is the internal phase-by-phase account of how
the 2026 document set was produced; its reader-facing derivatives are
[`CHANGELOG.md`](../CHANGELOG.md) (full history) and
[`WHATS-CHANGED.md`](../WHATS-CHANGED.md) (plain-language notice).
Self-review records (consistency reviews, watchlists) live in
[`reviews/`](../reviews/); decisions in [`decisions/`](../decisions/).
