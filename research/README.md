# research/

Background, drafting material, and durable rationale records behind the policy
documents — both pages we intend to add but have not yet published and the
research that shaped the published set. Held here so it stays out of the
top-level document set (which is reserved for the live legal documents) while
remaining versioned and easy to find.

- `business-arrangements-we-support.md` — draft of the "Business arrangements
  we support" page. Per decision BA-1 (see `../REMAINING_DECISIONS.md`) this is
  the sole home of several commitments (sponsored arrangements, payer
  decoupling, consolidated invoicing, alias/boundary domains, spin-out path);
  retain or re-home those when the page moves toward publication. Do not publish
  the "promote domain to organization" capability claim until it is
  code-verified.
- `business-arrangements-rationale.md` — the Kinde-informed pricing/arrangement
  rationale behind the page above (formerly `ERRATA-1.md`).
- `research-approximated-architecture.md` — background on the Approximated
  edge/Custom-Domains architecture, for the eventual per-region data-residency
  and edge-TLS documentation.
- `passphrase-crypto-rationale.md` — the codebase-verified cryptographic
  rationale behind the passphrase and compelled-disclosure wording in the PP
  and DPA Schedule C §1.1 (why "we cannot decrypt" was removed and why AAD
  binding cannot restore it).
- `errata-research-citations.md` — regulatory and prior-art citations behind
  the subprocessor change-notice design (EDPB Opinion 22/2024, Art. 28(2)) and
  the API-integration clause, preserved from the deleted ERRATA working files.
- `revision-history-2026.md` — internal phase-by-phase record of the 2026
  document revision with commit anchors (fork → 2024 baseline → the 2026
  waves); source material behind `../CHANGELOG.md` and `../WHATS-CHANGED.md`.
- `resale-prior-art.md` — June 2026 survey of how OSS/SaaS companies word
  resale, managed-service, and white-label terms; prior art for ToS B.3/C.4.
